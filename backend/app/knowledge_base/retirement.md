# Retirement & Legacy Planning (ریٹائرمنٹ اور وصیت)

The final stage of financial literacy is planning for your retirement years and managing the transition of your wealth to the next generation.

---

## 1. Planning for Retirement
Retirement planning involves building an asset base that can support your living expenses when you stop working.
* **The 4% Rule**: A guideline stating that you can safely withdraw 4% of your retirement portfolio annually without running out of money.
* **Target Capital**: You need approximately 25 times your annual expenses saved to retire comfortably.

---

## 2. Voluntary Pension Schemes (VPS) in Pakistan
Regulated by the SECP, **VPS** is a savings mechanism designed to build retirement funds:
* **Tax Rebates**: Contributions to VPS are eligible for tax credits (up to 20% of taxable income under Section 63).
* **Fund Managers**: Managed by major asset management companies (e.g. Meezan, NBP, Al Meezan).
* **Shariah Options**: Islamic pension funds invest purely in interest-free corporate bonds (Sukuks) and Islamic shares.

---

## 3. Legacy & Inheritance (وصیت اور وراثت)
Legacy planning ensures your assets are distributed smoothly according to your wishes and legal frameworks:
* **Wasiyat (Islamic Will)**: In Pakistan, a Muslim can write a will (Wasiyat) for up to **one-third (1/3)** of their wealth, to be given to non-heirs (e.g. charity, friends).
* **Inheritance Laws**: The remaining two-thirds of assets are automatically distributed among legal heirs (parents, spouse, children) according to Shariah inheritance rules.
