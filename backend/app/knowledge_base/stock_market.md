# Stock Market Basics (اسٹاک مارکیٹ)

The stock market allows individual investors to own fractions of publicly traded companies, participating in their growth and profits.

---

## 1. What is a Share / Stock?
A **Share (شیئر)** represents a unit of ownership in a company. If a company has 1,000 shares, and you buy 10 shares, you own 1% of that company.

---

## 2. The Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX)
In Pakistan, shares are bought and sold on the **Pakistan Stock Exchange (PSX)**.
* **KSE-100 Index**: The benchmark index of the PSX, tracking the performance of the 100 largest and most active companies. It serves as a health indicator of Pakistan's corporate economy.

---

## 3. How Do Investors Make Money?
1. **Capital Gains (منافع)**: Buying a share at a low price and selling it at a higher price.
2. **Dividends (منافع کا حصہ)**: Payments made by a company to its shareholders out of its profits.

---

## 4. Risks in Stock Trading
Stock prices fluctuate daily based on company news, economic policies, and investor sentiment.
* **Volatility**: High short-term price fluctuations.
* **Diversification Importance**: Investing in multiple sectors (banking, cement, energy) reduces the risk of a single stock collapse wiping out your savings.
