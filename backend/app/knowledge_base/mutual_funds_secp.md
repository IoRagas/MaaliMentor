# Mutual Funds in Pakistan — SECP Regulated Industry

Mutual fund ek aisa sarmayakari ka zariya hai jahan sainkron sarmayakar (investors) apna paisa ek jagah jama karte hain. Ek professional **Fund Manager** is poore pool ko stock market, sarkari bonds, sukuk, ya cash instruments mein invest karta hai. Pakistan mein is industry ko **Securities and Exchange Commission of Pakistan (SECP)** regulate karta hai.

---

## 1. NAV (Net Asset Value) Kya Hai aur Kaise Calulate Hoti Hai?

Mutual fund mein jab aap invest karte hain, toh aap ko shares ki jagah **Units** milte hain. Ek unit ki qeemat ko **NAV (Net Asset Value)** kehte hain.

### NAV Formula:
$$NAV = \frac{\text{Total Assets of the Fund} - \text{Total Liabilities}}{\text{Total Number of Outstanding Units}}$$

* **Assets**: Stock market mein lagaye gaye shares ki current market value, sarkari bonds ki value, aur cash balance.
* **Liabilities**: Management fee, custodian charges, aur digar expense ratio.
* **Misaal**: Agar ek fund ke paas total assets Rs. 1,05,00,000 hain aur liabilities Rs. 5,00,000 hain, aur total outstanding units 10,00,000 hain:
  $$NAV = \frac{1,05,00,000 - 5,00,000}{10,00,000} = \text{Rs. 10.00 per unit}$$
  Agar aap Rs. 50,000 invest karein ge, toh aap ko $50,000 / 10 = 5,000$ units milenge.

## 2. SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) aur Rupee Cost Averaging

**SIP** ka matlab hai har mahine bina nagha ek fixed amount (maslan Rs. 5,000) mutual fund mein invest karna. Yeh method stock market ki utaar-charhao se bachne ke liye behtareen hai, jise **Rupee Cost Averaging** kehte hain.

### Rupee Cost Averaging Misaal:
Soochiye aap har mahine Rs. 5,000 invest karte hain:

| Month | Monthly Investment | NAV (Per Unit) | Units Purchased |
|---|---|---|---|
| Month 1 | Rs. 5,000 | Rs. 10.00 | 500.0 units |
| Month 2 | Rs. 5,000 | Rs. 8.00 (Market drop) | 625.0 units |
| Month 3 | Rs. 5,000 | Rs. 12.50 (Market rise) | 400.0 units |
| **Total** | **Rs. 15,000** | **Average: Rs. 10.16** | **1,525.0 units** |

* **Total Portfolio Value**: $1,525 \times 12.50 = \text{Rs. 19,062.5}$
* **Total Profit**: Rs. 4,062.5 (27% Return)
* **Khaas Baat**: Jab market giri (Month 2), toh aap ke Rs. 5,000 ne zyada units (625) khareede. Jab market charhi, toh kam khareede. Isse aap ki per-unit cost average ho gayi!

Pakistan mein mukhtalif risk levels ke mutabiq funds available hain:

| Fund Type | Major Investments | Risk Level | Expected Return (Annual Average) | Recommended Timeline |

| **Money Market Funds** | Government T-Bills, Bank Deposits. | Very Low | 18% - 21% (In high policy rate periods) | Short term (1-6 months) |

## 5. Mutual Funds Par Tax aur Zakat Ke Qawaneen

### Capital Gains Tax (CGT)
Jab aap mutual fund bech kar profit kamate hain, toh uspar CGT lagta hai (Filer vs Non-Filer tax differential rules apply):
* **Filer**: Profit par **15%** tax lagta hai.
* **Non-Filer**: Profit par **30%** tax lagta hai.

### Dividend Tax
Agar fund aap ko monthly/quarterly dividend pay karta hai:
* **Filer**: Dividend income par **15%** tax cut hota hai.
* **Non-Filer**: Dividend income par **30%** tax cut hota hai.

### Zakat Deduction Rules
* 1st Ramzan ko default tor par har sarmayakar ke account se **2.5% Zakat** deduct hoti hai.
* **Zakat Exemption**: Agar aap Zakat deduct nahi karwana chahte, toh aap ko **CZ-50 Form** (Zakat Exemption Affidavit) stamp paper par AMC ke paas submit karna hota hai.

## 6. SECP Regulations aur Investor Protection

* **Custodian**: Investors ka paisa directly AMC ke paas nahi hota, balke ek azaad Custodian bank (jaise CDC - Central Depository Company) ke paas hota hai. Isliye fund manager paisa lekar bhaag nahi sakta.
* **Daily NAV Updates**: Har AMC par lazmi hai ke har working day ki raat 10 baje se pehle NAV update kare. Yeh updates **MUFAP (Mutual Funds Association of Pakistan)** ki official portal (mufap.com.pk) par bhi dekhi ja sakti hain.
* **SECP Sarmaaya Portal**: SECP ka official investor portal (sarmaaya.pk) jahan sarmayakar education aur guide haasil kar sakte hain.
