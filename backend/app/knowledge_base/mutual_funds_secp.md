# Mutual Funds in Pakistan — SECP Regulated

## Mutual Fund Kya Hai?

Mutual fund ek aisa tareeqa hai jismein bohut se logon ka paisa ikattha karke ek professional fund manager invest karta hai. Aap ko shares ki jagah **units** milte hain.

### Asan Misaal
Soochiye ke 100 log hain, har ek ke paas Rs.10,000 hain. Akele invest karna mushkil hai. Lekin sab ka paisa ikattha ho toh Rs.10,00,000 ban jata hai — isse ek mahir shakhs (fund manager) acha portfolio bana sakta hai.

## Fund Ki Qismein (Types of Mutual Funds)

### 1. Equity Funds (Hissas Funds)
- **Kya Karte Hain**: Stock market (PSX) ke shares mein invest karte hain
- **Risk**: Zyada
- **Expected Return**: 14-20% saalana (long term average)
- **Kis Ke Liye**: Jo 5+ saal ke liye invest kar sakte hain

### 2. Income / Bond Funds
- **Kya Karte Hain**: Government bonds, TFCs, sukuk mein invest karte hain
- **Risk**: Kam
- **Expected Return**: 10-14% saalana
- **Kis Ke Liye**: Conservative investors

### 3. Money Market Funds
- **Kya Karte Hain**: Short-term instruments (T-bills, deposits) mein invest karte hain
- **Risk**: Bahut kam
- **Expected Return**: 8-12% saalana
- **Kis Ke Liye**: Emergency fund rakhne ke liye

### 4. Balanced / Asset Allocation Funds
- **Kya Karte Hain**: Mix — kuch shares mein, kuch bonds mein
- **Risk**: Moderate
- **Expected Return**: 12-16% saalana
- **Kis Ke Liye**: Moderate risk appetite wale

### 5. Islamic Funds (Shariah-Compliant)
- Upar ki sab qismein Islamic version mein bhi available hain
- Shariah board monitor karta hai
- Sirf halal investments mein paisa lagaya jata hai

## SECP (Securities & Exchange Commission of Pakistan)

SECP mutual funds ko regulate karta hai. Yeh ensure karta hai ke:
- Fund managers registered aur qualified hon
- Investors ka paisa mehfooz ho
- NAV (Net Asset Value) daily publish ho
- Fund ka portfolio disclosed ho

### Key SECP Rules
- Minimum investment: Usually Rs.5,000 se Rs.10,000
- Redemption: Usually 2-3 business days mein paisa wapas
- Management fee: Usually 1-3% saalana
- Front-end load: 0-3% (kuch funds mein koi load nahi)

## Kaise Invest Karein?

### Step 1: AMC Chunein
- Website par jayein ya branch visit karein
- Online account bhi khul sakta hai (maslan Al-Meezan ka online portal)

### Step 2: KYC (Know Your Customer)
- CNIC copy
- Bank account details
- CNIC ke saath selfie (online ke liye)
- Rs.100 stamp paper (physical ke liye)

### Step 3: Investment Start Karein
- Minimum Rs.5,000 se Rs.10,000 se shuru
- Monthly SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) bhi available — Rs.1,000/month se

### Step 4: Monitor Karein
- Daily NAV check karein (AMC website ya MUFAP.com.pk)
- Quarterly statement milega

## Important Terms

- **NAV (Net Asset Value)**: Ek unit ki qeemat — daily change hoti hai
- **Load**: Entry ya exit fee — kuch funds mein nahi hoti
- **SIP**: Monthly thoda thoda invest karna — best strategy hai
- **Redemption**: Units bech kar paisa wapas lena
- **MUFAP**: Mutual Funds Association of Pakistan — industry body

## Tax on Mutual Funds
- Capital gains tax (CGT): Holding period ke hisaab se
  - 1 saal se kam: 15% (filer), 30% (non-filer)
  - 1-2 saal: 12.5% (filer), 25% (non-filer)
  - 2-4 saal: 10% (filer), 20% (non-filer)
  - 4+ saal: 0% (both)
- Dividend: 15% (filer), 30% (non-filer)
