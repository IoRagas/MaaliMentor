# Investing Principles (سرمایہ کاری کے اصول)

Investing is the process of putting your saved money into financial assets with the expectation of generating profit or income over time.

---

## 1. Saving vs. Investing
* **Saving**: Setting money aside in a safe place for short-term needs (e.g. cash, saving account). High safety, low returns.
* **Investing**: Committing capital to assets like mutual funds, stocks, or real estate for long-term growth. Higher volatility, but higher returns that beat inflation.

---

## 2. Risk and Return Tradeoff
There is a direct correlation between risk and potential return:
* **Low Risk**: Low potential returns (e.g., cash, saving accounts).
* **Medium Risk**: Moderate returns (e.g., mutual funds, gold).
* **High Risk**: High potential returns, but high risk of losses (e.g., individual stock trading, cryptocurrencies).

---

## 3. The Power of Compounding (مرکب سود)
Compounding is earning return on your previously earned returns. Over a long period (10-20 years), compounding creates exponential wealth growth.
* **Formula**:
  $$A = P \left(1 + \frac{r}{n}\right)^{nt}$$

---

## 4. Key Investing Rules for Beginners
1. **Invest early**: Gives compounding more time to work.
2. **Diversify**: Don't put all your eggs in one basket.
3. **Patience**: Avoid panic selling during short-term market dips.
