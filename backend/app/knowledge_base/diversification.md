# Diversification & Rebalancing (اثاثوں کی تقسیم)

Diversification is the golden rule of portfolio management. Spreading your investments minimizes risk while optimizing overall returns.

---

## 1. What is Diversification?
**Diversification (تنوع)** means spreading your savings across different asset classes (cash, bank savings, stocks, gold, real estate) so that a poor performance in one asset is offset by a strong performance in another.

> **"Don't put all your eggs in one basket."**

---

## 2. Correlation Between Assets
Different assets behave differently during economic events:
* When inflation rises, **Cash** loses purchasing power, but **Gold** and **Real Estate** values typically increase.
* During stock market declines, **Bank Savings** remain stable, protecting your capital.

---

## 3. Asset Allocation Strategies
Your asset mix depends on your age, risk tolerance, and goals:
* **Aggressive (Young Investors)**: 70% Equities (Stocks/Mutual Funds), 20% Cash/Savings, 10% Gold.
* **Balanced**: 40% Equities, 40% Savings/Islamic Funds, 20% Gold/Real Estate.
* **Conservative (Close to Retirement)**: 20% Equities, 60% Savings/Fixed income, 20% Gold/Cash.

---

## 4. Portfolio Rebalancing
Over time, some assets grow faster than others, changing your initial asset mix. **Rebalancing** is the process of buying or selling assets to restore your target allocation. It forces you to sell high-performing assets (locking in profit) and buy underperforming assets (buying low).
