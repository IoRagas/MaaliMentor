# Gold, Property & Alternatives (سونا اور رئیل اسٹیٹ)

Tangible, real assets like gold and real estate are highly popular wealth preservation vehicles in Pakistan, offering stability during periods of extreme inflation and currency devaluation.

---

## 1. Gold as an Inflation Hedge
In Pakistan, **Gold (سونا)** is a traditional and highly trusted form of saving.
* **Safe Haven**: When the PKR devalues or inflation rises, the local price of gold tends to increase, preserving your real purchasing power.
* **Tola System**: Locally, gold is traded in Tolas (1 Tola = 11.66 grams).
* **Liquidity**: Gold is highly liquid; it can be sold instantly for cash at any jewelry market.

---

## 2. Real Estate Investing
Real estate involves purchasing land, commercial property, or residential homes.
* **Capital Appreciation**: The value of land in Pakistan has historically risen due to population growth and urbanization.
* **Rental Yield**: Generating monthly income by leasing the property.
* **REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts)**: A way to invest in large-scale real estate projects without buying physical property. You buy shares of a REIT on the stock market (regulated by SECP).

---

## 3. Alternative Investments
Other tangible options include:
* **Commodities**: Trading agricultural goods or metals.
* **SME Business Investment**: Partnering in local small-scale businesses for profit sharing.
